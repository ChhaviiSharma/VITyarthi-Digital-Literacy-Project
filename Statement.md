PROBLEM STATEMENT-

In this project we need to complete 5 tasks mentioned below:

Task 1-Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

Task 2-Student Digital Portfolio

Task 3-Coding And Collaboration Platforms

Task 4-Professional Email and Etiquette Guide

Task 5-Cybercrime Awareness Case Study and Prevention Guide


SCOPE OF THE PROJECT-

Digital literacy is an essential skill for students in today’s technology-driven world.

It helps individuals use digital tools effectively, communicate professionally, and stay safe online.

This project was designed to develop awareness about digital platforms, professional communication, and cyber safety. 

As part of the assignment, I completed five different tasks that focused on creating an infographic, building a digital portfolio, exploring coding platforms,
drafting professional emails, and understanding cybercrime.

TARGET- 

To help students develop their skills and know how to use digital platforms such as Canva, coding platforms such as Hacker Rank , Kaggle etc and know about cyber crimes in advance so that they do not fall in the trap of digital crimes .
