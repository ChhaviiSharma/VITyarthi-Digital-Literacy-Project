Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)


1. Keep social media accounts private – Limit who can see your posts, stories, and personal details to prevent misuse.

2. Avoid sharing personal information – Do not post your phone number, address, college ID, or daily routine publicly.

3. Think before posting – Once shared online, content can be copied, edited, and spread without your control.

4. Block and report abusive users immediately – Do not engage with cyberbullies; use platform tools to block them.

5. Use strong passwords and enable two factor authentication – This prevents unauthorized access to your accounts.

6. UPI Safety Tip - Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.

7. UPI Safety Tip - Do not approve “collect requests” on apps like PhonePe, Google Pay, or Paytm unless you personally know the sender.

8. Save evidence of cyberbullying – Take screenshots of messages, posts, or profiles for reporting.

9. Avoid clicking suspicious links – They may lead to phishing pages or malware that can steal your data.

10. Talk to a trusted person – Inform friends, teachers, or family if you experience online harassment.


Reporting Channels in India:

* National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
